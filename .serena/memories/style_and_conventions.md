Backend conventions: PascalCase for types/methods/properties, camelCase for locals/parameters, interfaces prefixed with I, async methods suffixed Async, file-scoped namespaces, nullable enabled. Follow layered architecture and existing exception/logging patterns. Services are the primary logging layer with structured identifiers; repositories should generally avoid catch-log-rethrow. Controllers follow existing try/catch HTTP mapping patterns and rely on global exception handling for uncaught errors.
